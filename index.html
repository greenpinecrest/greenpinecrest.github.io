from Principal's Desk

Mr. Atanu Mahata

Principal

“DON’T WAIT FOR THE STARS TO ALIGN REACH UP AND ARRANGE THEM THE WAY YOU WANT”

The school aims to provide a holistic approach to shape the overall personality of a child imbibed in the moral values. The school has a modern outlook based on the strong roots of our traditional culture. It is matter of great contentment that school has progressed in all fields in the recent years. Parents are requested to extend their whole hearted cooperation to the school authorities for the success of the students and school.
Let us pave the way to its commencement with great fervor and supposition for achieving towering standards in the field of academics and other essential skills .
“Education awakens the power and beauty that lie within us.” Education does not only mean academic excellence. It rather is a harmonious and synchronized combination of hand ( skills like various arts ) , head ( intellectual Power ) and heart ( Value System ) . In the present era of the digitized world, it is the biggest challenge before educators and parents, to nurture the young minds with the indelible impressions of holistic education.
Therefore, we come up with a vision to foster different facets of a student in order to see him/her developing as a vibrant student , responsible citizen and above all a generous and sentient human being. Our pedagogy is child centric, with emphasis of over-all growth and development of our students.
We believe that true wisdom is the ability to listen to your heart and that each child’s self esteem, dignity, physical and emotional well being must be cultivated with the same importance as that given to academic and material success. The educational needs of today are very different from those of the past because of growing competition and with the progress of science and technology. The students must be equipped with the skills required to face the challenges of the future, they can do it easily if we can make the surroundings stimulating and purposeful.
